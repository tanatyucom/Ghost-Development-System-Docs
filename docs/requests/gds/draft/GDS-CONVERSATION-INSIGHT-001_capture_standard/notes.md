# Notes

## Scope

- Target Project: Ghost Development System.
- Task: Conversation Insight Capture Standard.
- Commit: Do not commit.

## Design Decision

Conversation Insight is a pre-promotion Knowledge Source, not a Rule.

AI may suggest candidates, but Human Approval is required before draft
generation or repository storage.

## Boundary

Conversation Insight does not replace:

- Q.
- Research Mission.
- CASE.
- Completion Report.
- Knowledge Inventory.

It captures important design or operation insight that would otherwise remain
buried in chat history.
