## Background

Roadmap2 review work often became clearer when candidates were placed side by
side instead of reviewed one at a time.

## Problem

Single-candidate review can hide relative weaknesses and make selection
decisions harder.

## Proposed Idea

When selecting between candidates, compare them side by side with the same
review criteria.

## Expected Benefit

This improves human review speed and decision consistency.

## Validation Plan

workflow variants.
