## Background

Roadmap2 investigations narrowed broad symptoms into smaller, reviewable
questions before deciding changes.

## Problem

Large ambiguous issues encourage broad fixes and unclear verification.

## Proposed Idea

Narrow an issue step by step from broad symptom to specific broken condition.

## Expected Benefit

This makes investigations easier to review and prevents overbroad changes.

## Validation Plan

Use in Q decomposition, audit before repair, and debug escalation.
