## Candidates

| Concept | Meaning | Candidate Destination |
|---|---|---|
| Roadmap2 Becomes History | Roadmap2 should stop being the active source after all reusable knowledge is migrated. | History / PIP |
| GDS Becomes Single Knowledge Base | GDS Knowledge Base becomes the active reference after salvage completion. | docs/README / PIP |
| Knowledge Salvage Loop | Repeat review, extraction, Q, update, and re-review until no missing knowledge remains. | Workflow / Rule |
| Missing Knowledge Extraction | Review output should identify missing reusable knowledge before new Q creation. | Workflow |
| Salvage Completion Condition | Completion requires no remaining Roadmap2-only reusable knowledge. | Rule |
| Concept Candidate | A reusable idea that is not yet specific enough to become CASE, Best Practice, Evolution, Investigation, or Rule Story. | PIP Concepts |
